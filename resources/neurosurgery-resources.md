# 📚 Top 100 Neurosurgery Resources

Curated textbooks, atlases, platforms, journals, and tools for surgical case preparation. Each entry links to the **official source** (for online platforms/journals) or to a **find‑the‑PDF search + a WorldCat library lookup** (for books) — use your **institution's library / Clinical Key / OvidSP / publisher access** to download full text behind a paywall.

> 📥 **Accessing paywalled books/articles:** sign in through your hospital or university library proxy (e.g., *library.youruniversity.edu* → databases → ClinicalKey / AccessSurgery / Ovid), or request via interlibrary loan. The 🔎 link finds an open PDF when one exists.

## 📕 Textbooks (20)

- **Cranial Anatomy and Surgical Approaches** · *Albert L. Rhoton Jr.* — The definitive atlas of cranial microsurgical anatomy. Essential for understanding every cranial approach, vascular relationships, and cranial nerve anatomy from the surgical perspective.  ([🔎 find/PDF](https://www.google.com/search?q=Cranial+Anatomy+and+Surgical+Approaches+Albert+L.+Rhoton+Jr.) · [📚 library](https://search.worldcat.org/search?q=Cranial+Anatomy+and+Surgical+Approaches+Albert+L.+Rhoton+Jr.))
  <br><span style="color:#5b6b7b">Why essential: Gold standard for surgical anatomy. Every approach chapter should reference Rhoton.</span>
- **Microneurosurgery (Volumes I-IV)** · *M. Gazi Yasargil* — Foundational 4-volume work on microsurgical technique. Vol I: Microsurgical Anatomy. Vol II: Clinical Considerations/Surgery of Intracranial Aneurysms and Results. Vol IIIA: AVM of the Brain. Vol IVB: Microneurosurgery of CNS Tumors.  ([🔎 find/PDF](https://www.google.com/search?q=Microneurosurgery+%28Volumes+I-IV%29+M.+Gazi+Yasargil) · [📚 library](https://search.worldcat.org/search?q=Microneurosurgery+%28Volumes+I-IV%29+M.+Gazi+Yasargil))
  <br><span style="color:#5b6b7b">Why essential: Yasargil pioneered modern microsurgery. His technique descriptions remain the benchmark.</span>
- **Handbook of Neurosurgery** · *Mark S. Greenberg* — Comprehensive single-volume reference covering all of neurosurgery. Quick reference for clinical decision-making, grading scales, dosing, and management algorithms.  ([🔎 find/PDF](https://www.google.com/search?q=Handbook+of+Neurosurgery+Mark+S.+Greenberg) · [📚 library](https://search.worldcat.org/search?q=Handbook+of+Neurosurgery+Mark+S.+Greenberg))
  <br><span style="color:#5b6b7b">Why essential: The 'bible' of neurosurgery for quick reference and board prep.</span>
- **Youmans & Winn Neurological Surgery** · *H. Richard Winn (ed.)* — Multi-volume comprehensive neurosurgery reference. Covers basic science, clinical evaluation, and all operative subspecialties in depth.  ([🔎 find/PDF](https://www.google.com/search?q=Youmans+%26+Winn+Neurological+Surgery+H.+Richard+Winn+%28ed.%29) · [📚 library](https://search.worldcat.org/search?q=Youmans+%26+Winn+Neurological+Surgery+H.+Richard+Winn+%28ed.%29))
  <br><span style="color:#5b6b7b">Why essential: Most comprehensive multi-author reference in the field.</span>
- **Schmidek & Sweet: Operative Neurosurgical Techniques** · *Alfredo Quinones-Hinojosa (ed.)* — Definitive operative techniques reference with step-by-step surgical descriptions, indications, and outcomes for virtually every neurosurgical procedure.  ([🔎 find/PDF](https://www.google.com/search?q=Schmidek+%26+Sweet%3A+Operative+Neurosurgical+Techniques+Alfredo+Quinones-Hinojosa+%28ed.%29) · [📚 library](https://search.worldcat.org/search?q=Schmidek+%26+Sweet%3A+Operative+Neurosurgical+Techniques+Alfredo+Quinones-Hinojosa+%28ed.%29))
  <br><span style="color:#5b6b7b">Why essential: Go-to for step-by-step operative technique with detailed illustrations.</span>
- **[Operative Neurosurgery (2 Volumes) ↗](https://academic.oup.com/ons)** · *John E. Kempe* — Classic operative atlas with detailed surgical illustrations of cranial and spinal procedures.
  <br><span style="color:#5b6b7b">Why essential: Beautifully illustrated classic operative reference.</span>
- **Atlas of Neurosurgical Techniques (2 Volumes)** · *Laligam N. Sekhar, Richard G. Fessler* — Vol 1: Brain. Vol 2: Spine and Peripheral Nerves. Comprehensive atlas with modern techniques and step-by-step approaches.  ([🔎 find/PDF](https://www.google.com/search?q=Atlas+of+Neurosurgical+Techniques+%282+Volumes%29+Laligam+N.+Sekhar%2C+Richard+G.+Fessler) · [📚 library](https://search.worldcat.org/search?q=Atlas+of+Neurosurgical+Techniques+%282+Volumes%29+Laligam+N.+Sekhar%2C+Richard+G.+Fessler))

- **Seven Aneurysms: Tenets and Techniques for Clipping** · *Michael T. Lawton* — Expert guide to aneurysm clipping organized by aneurysm morphology and location. Includes decision-making frameworks and clip selection strategies.  ([🔎 find/PDF](https://www.google.com/search?q=Seven+Aneurysms%3A+Tenets+and+Techniques+for+Clipping+Michael+T.+Lawton) · [📚 library](https://search.worldcat.org/search?q=Seven+Aneurysms%3A+Tenets+and+Techniques+for+Clipping+Michael+T.+Lawton))

- **Seven AVMs: Tenets and Techniques for Resection** · *Michael T. Lawton* — Companion to Seven Aneurysms focused on AVM resection. Organized by AVM location and angioarchitecture.  ([🔎 find/PDF](https://www.google.com/search?q=Seven+AVMs%3A+Tenets+and+Techniques+for+Resection+Michael+T.+Lawton) · [📚 library](https://search.worldcat.org/search?q=Seven+AVMs%3A+Tenets+and+Techniques+for+Resection+Michael+T.+Lawton))
  <br><span style="color:#5b6b7b">Why essential: Definitive modern guide to AVM surgery.</span>
- **Meningiomas** · *Ossama Al-Mefty* — Comprehensive reference on meningioma surgery including skull base meningiomas, with detailed approach-specific chapters.  ([🔎 find/PDF](https://www.google.com/search?q=Meningiomas+Ossama+Al-Mefty) · [📚 library](https://search.worldcat.org/search?q=Meningiomas+Ossama+Al-Mefty))
  <br><span style="color:#5b6b7b">Why essential: The authoritative text on meningioma management and surgical technique.</span>
- **Neurovascular Surgery** · *Robert F. Spetzler, M. Yashar S. ([🔎 find/PDF](https://www.google.com/search?q=Surgery+of+the+Third+Ventricle+Michael+L.J.+Apuzzo) · [📚 library](https://search.worldcat.org/search?q=Surgery+of+the+Third+Ventricle+Michael+L.J.+Apuzzo))

- **Fundamentals of Operative Techniques in Neurosurgery** · *E. Sander Connolly Jr., Guy M. - **Principles of Neurosurgery** · *Setti S. Rengachary, Richard G. Ellenbogen* — Well-organized principles-based neurosurgery text covering fundamental concepts and clinical practice.  ([🔎 find/PDF](https://www.google.com/search?q=Principles+of+Neurosurgery+Setti+S.+Rengachary%2C+Richard+G.+Ellenbogen) · [📚 library](https://search.worldcat.org/search?q=Principles+of+Neurosurgery+Setti+S.+Rengachary%2C+Richard+G.+Ellenbogen))

## 🧭 Approach References (20)

- **Pterional (Frontotemporal) Craniotomy** · *Multiple (Yasargil, Rhoton, Cohen-Gadol)* — The workhorse cranial approach. Access to anterior and middle cranial fossa, circle of Willis, sellar/parasellar region, and lateral skull base.  ([🔎 find/PDF](https://www.google.com/search?q=Pterional+%28Frontotemporal%29+Craniotomy+Multiple+%28Yasargil%2C+Rhoton%2C+Cohen-Gadol%29) · [📚 library](https://search.worldcat.org/search?q=Pterional+%28Frontotemporal%29+Craniotomy+Multiple+%28Yasargil%2C+Rhoton%2C+Cohen-Gadol%29))
  <br><span style="color:#5b6b7b">Why essential: Most commonly used cranial approach — must master completely.</span>
- **Retrosigmoid (Retromastoid) Craniotomy** · *Multiple (Samii, Rhoton, Sekhar)* — Posterior fossa approach for CPA lesions, petroclival tumors, and microvascular decompression.  ([🔎 find/PDF](https://www.google.com/search?q=Retrosigmoid+%28Retromastoid%29+Craniotomy+Multiple+%28Samii%2C+Rhoton%2C+Sekhar%29) · [📚 library](https://search.worldcat.org/search?q=Retrosigmoid+%28Retromastoid%29+Craniotomy+Multiple+%28Samii%2C+Rhoton%2C+Sekhar%29))
  <br><span style="color:#5b6b7b">Why essential: Primary approach for CPA pathology and MVD.</span>
- **Suboccipital Midline Craniotomy** · *Multiple* — Posterior fossa midline approach for cerebellar and fourth ventricle pathology.  ([🔎 find/PDF](https://www.google.com/search?q=Suboccipital+Midline+Craniotomy+Multiple) · [📚 library](https://search.worldcat.org/search?q=Suboccipital+Midline+Craniotomy+Multiple))
